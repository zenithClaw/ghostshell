GhostShell is the first context-aware "ghost" layer for your desktop. Sticky notes that float, windows that hide in plain sight, and logic that stays with your screen.

Get GhostShell

Context-Aware Tabs

Windows don't just minimize—they transform. Push any window to the screen edge and watch it become a minimal, glowing tab. One click to resurrect, zero clutter in your workspace.

Ultra Low-Latency

Built with native C bindings. GhostShell interacts directly with your OS window manager for near-zero CPU footprint.

The Sticky Ghost

A transparent overlay for your thoughts. Write temporary notes, hex codes, or logic steps that stay visible regardless of which app you switch to.

Multi-Display Continuity

GhostShell remembers your specific layout for every monitor setup. Plug in your HDMI and watch your invisible interface restore itself instantly, down to the last coordinate.
